Meatballs

A simple classic swedish way of making the famous swedish Meatballs.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef 1 kg
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 kg potatos
  • 1 dl of heavy cream
  • 1/2 dl of milk
  • Butter 50g
  • Soy sauce 1 teaspoon

Directions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Boil potatos for about 20 minutes or until soft

  2. Step 2:

    3. Finely slice onion and mix together with the ground beef, add white pepper and salt

  3. Step 3:

    4. Roll the mixed ground beef into your preferred size of the meatballs

  4. Step 4:

    5. Add some butter in the pan and cook them until brown

  5. Step 5:

    6. When the meatballs are done, take the meatballs out and leave them on a plate, add heavy cream into the pan and add the soy sauce, simmer on medium high heat

  6. Step 6:

    7. When the potatos are done, pour out the water and mash the potatos. Add the milk and butter and mix together on low heat.
