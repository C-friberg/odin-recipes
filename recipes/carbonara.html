Carbonara

A creamy carbonara that requires minimal effort, delicious both on a weekday or as a weekend meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • Pancetta 300g
  • Parmigiano reggiano 2 dl
  • Heavy cream 1 dl
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Boil pasta after directions until al dente

  2. Step 2:

    3. Mix parmigiano with cream, black pepper, eggs, and salt

  3. Step 3:

    4. Cook the pancetta on medium heat until crispy

  4. Step 4:

    5. Mix everything together and let it cook on low heat until it gets thicker.

  5. Step 5:

    6. In a bowl, add some parmigiano and black pepper then serve
